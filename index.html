Yours to Play and Win
This is Thomas' diploma project at ECAL. The project starts with the type design of the Crowlista Mono family. It is promoted on the theme of chess, using it as a typographical and conceptual support. The font is optimised for the game, inculding an optimised algebraic notation stylistic set. A reflection on symbols, their activation, and the ideographical representation of abstract images and ideas runs throughout the project, in reference to Marcel Duchamp who compared the chess pieces to a "block alphabet which shapes thoughts". Following this idea, the glyphs can replaces the chess symbols on the checker. However, the idea runs further and the edition develops representations and experimentations on this idea. A website allows a dynamic and interactive showcase, while the edition's packaging, a box serving as a chessmat, materialises the concept.
Coemeter
Coemeter offers a reinterpretation of the Trajan typeface. This typographic work is showcased on a website. A digital cemetery awaits, where you can engrave your tombstone and personalize your mourning space.

Coemeter plays with dualities, between the sacred and the profane, history and anachronism, matter and consciousness. This unpretentious and poetic experience invites us to reconsider our connection to stone, memory, and loss.
